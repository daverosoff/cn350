Activity 2.3.4.

Consider the following \(\varepsilon\)-NFA.
\(\varepsilon\) \(a\) \(b\) \(c\)
\(\to p\) \(\varnothing\) \(\{p\}\) \(\{q\}\) \(\{r\}\)
\(q\) \(\{p\}\) \(\{q\}\) \(\{r\}\) \(\varnothing\)
\(\ast r\) \(\{q\}\) \(\{r\}\) \(\varnothing\) \(\{p\}\)
  1. Compute the \(\varepsilon\)-closure of each state.
  2. Give all the strings of length 3 or less computed by the automaton.
