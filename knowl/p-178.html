We introduce pseudo-NFA in part to show that they are just as sensible as NFA (indeed some authors only consider pseudo-NFA). In particular, we show in TheoremÂ 2.3.10 that the subset construction may be applied to a pseudo-NFA to produce a DFA that accepts the same language.
